About Fly2Work

Fly2Work was designed from the ground up specifically to address the issue around managing travellers to and from worksites, typically where the form of transport and accommodation can’t be booked through one central system.

Fly2Work is an inventory distribution platform with the option for travellers and coordinators to easily make and change bookings online.

With proven success in consolidating processes and removing manual intervention, Fly2Work can be used by customers to take control of their site travel requirements, or for suppliers to utilise the system as an inexpensive option for their charter and accommodation requirements.

We are not a Travel Management Company (TMC) that specialises in commercial travel, but we can work in partnership with a TMC to provide a totally comprehensive system.

Tailored for the customer with options to integrate with internal or supplier systems, Fly2Work offers a solution for everyone that can dramatically reduce costs and ultimately bring back control over your travel requirements.

Fly2Work in 4 easy steps

+ Booking

Traveller or coordinator access and book air and ground transport, plus site accommodation. Option for users to make multiple bookings in the one transaction

+ Authorising

Traveller or coordinator access and book air and ground transport, plus site accommodation. Option for users to make multiple bookings in the one transaction

+ Travel & Accom

Traveller receive automatic detailed itinerary or can access live through Fly2Work. Quick view of all confirmed travel via Calendar and access to booking if changes required. Travellers can receive SMS and email notifications for alerts or updates

+ Reporting

Real-time rich data on traveller segments including specific company information such as cost codes, department etc. Reports can be run on historical or future travel, which allows companies to effectively manage schedules and inventory

+ Booking

Traveller have 24/7 access through any internet enabled device to view itinerary either through calendar icon or itinerary. If permitted, traveller can make changes to bookings at any time through itinerary link in calendar. Company can message and alert travellers before and during site trip.
